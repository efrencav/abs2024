HOW WE WORK

OUR DESIGN PROCESS THAT WORKS

01

Research

This is the first phase where the designer conducts research to better understand the client's needs, goals, and competitors. This phase involves gathering information about the project's target audience, the industry, and the project's scope and limitations. The designer may also conduct surveys, interviews, and focus groups to gain additional insights.

02

Conceptualization

Once the research phase is complete, the designer moves on to the conceptualization phase. This phase involves brainstorming ideas and developing a creative strategy that aligns with the project's goals. The designer may sketch out rough ideas and create mood boards to explore different color palettes, typography, and visual styles.

03

Design Development

In this phase, the designer takes the concepts from the previous phase and starts to develop them into final designs. The designer will create multiple design iterations and refine them based on feedback from the client and their own design expertise. This phase may also involve creating prototypes or mockups to help the client visualize how the final product will look.

04

Implementation

The final phase involves implementing the design across various mediums, such as print and digital media. The designer will work closely with the client to ensure that the final product meets their expectations and is delivered on time and within budget. This phase may also involve creating brand guidelines or style guides to ensure that future designs adhere to the established design standards.
